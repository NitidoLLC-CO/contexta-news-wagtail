Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how CONTEXTRA News Agency collects, uses, stores, shares, and protects personal information when people use CONTEXTRA News, contact our newsroom, subscribe to our briefings, or interact with our digital intelligence-news services.

1. Who we are

CONTEXTRA News Agency, operating from Dubai, United Arab Emirates, publishes news, analysis, briefings, and intelligence-style reporting through CONTEXTRA News and related digital channels. For the purposes of this Privacy Policy, "CONTEXTRA", "we", "us", and "our" mean CONTEXTRA News Agency.

For privacy questions, requests, or complaints, contact us at info@contextra.news.

2. Scope of this policy

This policy applies to personal information processed through our website, newsletters, forms, editorial communications, business communications, advertising or sponsorship inquiries, audience analytics, and security systems. It does not cover websites, platforms, or services operated by third parties that may be linked from our articles or pages.

3. Information we collect

We may collect the following categories of personal information:

  • Contact information: name, email address, organization, job title, and message content when you contact us or subscribe.
  • Account or preference information: newsletter preferences, saved interests, alert preferences, and communication choices where those features are offered.
  • Technical information: IP address, device type, browser, operating system, approximate location derived from IP address, pages viewed, referring links, and log data.
  • Editorial communications: information provided to our newsroom, including tips, source communications, documents, and related metadata.
  • Business information: information submitted by advertisers, partners, vendors, contributors, applicants, or other professional contacts.

We do not intentionally collect sensitive personal information unless you voluntarily provide it, it is necessary for journalistic/editorial purposes, or we are required or permitted to process it under applicable law.

4. How we use information

We use personal information for the following purposes:

  • To operate, secure, maintain, and improve CONTEXTRA News.
  • To publish, manage, and support our editorial and news operations.
  • To respond to messages, tips, requests, and business inquiries.
  • To send newsletters, briefings, alerts, or updates when you request them.
  • To understand audience engagement and improve content, layout, and performance.
  • To prevent fraud, abuse, spam, scraping, unauthorized access, and security incidents.
  • To comply with legal obligations and enforce our rights, terms, and policies.

5. Legal bases for processing

Where applicable data protection law requires a legal basis, we rely on one or more of the following: your consent, performance of a contract or requested service, compliance with legal obligations, our legitimate interests in operating a secure news service, and public-interest or journalistic/editorial purposes where recognized by law.

6. Cookies, analytics, and similar technologies

We may use cookies, pixels, local storage, server logs, and similar technologies to operate the site, remember preferences, measure performance, understand audience behavior, detect abuse, and improve our services.

Some cookies are necessary for security and site operation. Other analytics or marketing technologies, if used, may be subject to consent controls where required by law. You can also control cookies through your browser settings.

7. Newsroom, sources, and editorial material

If you contact our newsroom, send a tip, or provide editorial material, we may process the information you provide for newsgathering, verification, publication, legal review, public-interest reporting, archival, and security purposes. We take source confidentiality seriously, but ordinary email is not always secure. Do not send highly sensitive information by email unless you understand and accept that risk.

8. Sharing information

We may share personal information with:

  • Service providers that host, secure, analyze, deliver, or support our website and communications.
  • Professional advisers, legal counsel, auditors, insurers, and compliance providers.
  • Public authorities, regulators, courts, or law enforcement where required by law or necessary to protect rights and safety.
  • Successors in connection with a merger, acquisition, restructuring, financing, or transfer of business assets.

We do not sell personal information in the ordinary sense. If future advertising or analytics practices are treated as a "sale" or "sharing" under a specific law, we will provide any required choices.

9. International transfers

CONTEXTRA is based in Dubai, UAE, and our service providers, readers, partners, and infrastructure may be located in other countries. Where personal information is transferred internationally, we use appropriate contractual, organizational, and technical safeguards where required by applicable law.

10. Your privacy rights

Depending on your location and the law that applies to you, you may have the right to request access to your personal information, correction, deletion, restriction, objection to processing, withdrawal of consent, portability, or cessation of certain processing. You may also have rights concerning automated decision-making where applicable.

To exercise a privacy right, email info@contextra.news. We may need to verify your identity and request additional information before completing your request. We will respond within the timeframe required by applicable law.

You may also have the right to complain to a competent data protection regulator or authority in your jurisdiction.

11. Retention

We keep personal information only for as long as reasonably necessary for the purposes described in this policy, including site operation, editorial archives, security, legal compliance, dispute resolution, and legitimate business records. Editorial records and published material may be retained for archival, public-interest, accountability, and historical purposes.

12. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures designed to protect personal information against unauthorized access, loss, misuse, alteration, and disclosure. No system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee that information transmitted through the internet will always remain secure.

13. Children

CONTEXTRA News is intended for a general professional and public audience and is not directed to children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16. If you believe a child has provided personal information to us, contact us and we will review the matter.

14. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date shows when this version became effective. Material changes will be posted on this page and, where appropriate, communicated through additional notice.
